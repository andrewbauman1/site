I bridge business and technology at the executive level, both for the firms I work with and for my own. I lead cross-firm project teams for hedge fund and private equity clients, working directly with CEOs, CFOs, CTOs, and Investor Relations teams to get complex initiatives across the finish line.

Internally, I help senior leadership shape how AI and other emerging technology fit into our products and client service. I'm also the filter between product and client, turning real use cases into recommendations our developers and product owners can act on. Right now, that includes building AI-powered automation that cuts busywork out of team reporting.

Outside of work, I cook a lot of Mediterranean food and bake, especially my family's German Buttercake, a personal specialty of mine. My husband and I travel when we can, always looking for the next place to explore. Our corn snake, Cornelius, has been part of the household for years. These days, the three of us are the reigning Jeopardy champions of the living room.
